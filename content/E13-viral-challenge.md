# Viral Challenge Campaign

## "Post Your First Job" Challenge

---

### Campaign Overview

**Name:** #MyFirstFreelanceJob Challenge
**Platform:** X (Twitter), Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn
**Duration:** 30 days
**Budget:** R50,000 (R100 credit x 500 participants)
**Goal:** 5,000 new job posts, 10,000 new signups

---

### The Hook

**"Post your first job on FreelanceSkills — tag @FreelanceSkills for R100 credit."**

That's it. Simple. Viral. Valuable.

---

### How It Works

1. **User signs up** at freelanceskills.net (free)
2. **Posts their first job** (any category: need a plumber? developer? tutor? photographer?)
3. **Screenshots the job post** and shares on social media
4. **Tags @FreelanceSkills** + uses hashtag **#MyFirstFreelanceJob**
5. **We verify** the post is real (automated check via API)
6. **R100 credit** added to their account within 24 hours
7. **Bonus R50** if their post gets 10+ likes/shares

---

### Social Media Templates

**For Clients:**
"Just posted my first job on @FreelanceSkills — looking for a [job type] in [city]. Let's see the AI magic work! #MyFirstFreelanceJob"

**For Freelancers:**
"I signed up on @FreelanceSkills and already found 3 jobs in my area. SA's got a proper freelance platform now! #MyFirstFreelanceJob"

---

### Influencer Seeding (Week 1)

Send 10 micro-influencers (5K-50K followers) a brief:
- Post their own job (real or sample)
- Film a 30-sec reaction to the AI matching
- Share with #MyFirstFreelanceJob
- Each gets R500 credit + affiliate link (R50/signup)

---

### Viral Mechanics

| Mechanic | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Low barrier | Free signup, free job post |
| Instant reward | R100 credit (not a raffle — guaranteed) |
| Social proof | See others posting jobs = FOMO |
| Network effect | Every job post = at least 1 new user sees the platform |
| Shareable | Screenshot of job post = organic content |
| Escalation | "Tag 3 friends who need a freelancer" for R50 bonus |
