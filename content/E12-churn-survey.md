# Churn Prevention Survey — 30 Days Inactive

---

## Trigger Conditions

- User has not logged in for 30+ days
- User had at least 1 completed project or 3+ profile views
- User has not already received this survey in the last 90 days

## Email Sequence

**Body:**

[CTA Button: "See My Opportunities"]
