# 30-Second Teaser Video Script
## "Work Without Borders"

---

### Production Notes
- **Format**: 30 seconds, 1080x1920 (vertical/Reels) + 1920x1080 (horizontal/YouTube)
- **Music**: Upbeat Afrobeats fusion, building energy, drops at :15
- **Pace**: Fast cuts (1-2 sec each), kinetic typography, split screens

---

### Script + Shot List

**[0:00-0:03] — HOOK**
*Screen: Black. Text slams in: "WHAT IF..."*
*Cut: Montage — hands typing, plumber fixing pipe, designer on tablet*
VO: "What if every skill had a stage?"

**[0:03-0:06] — THE PROBLEM**
*Screen: Stats fly in with urgency*
"32% unemployment. 60% youth. But talent? Talent is everywhere."
*Visual: Map of South Africa, dots lighting up in every city*

**[0:06-0:10] — THE PLATFORM**
*Cut: FreelanceSkills dashboard — jobs scrolling, profiles loading*
VO: "Meet FreelanceSkills."
*Quick cuts: Job posted → AI match → Freelancer notified → Chat opens*

**[0:10-0:14] — AI MATCHING**
*Screen: AI visualization — neural network matching talent to jobs*
VO: "AI that matches the right person to the right job."
*Visual: Developer in Sandton matched with startup in London. Plumber in Soweto matched with homeowner in Randburg.*

**[0:14-0:18] — SECURE PAYMENT**
*Screen: Payment flow animation — client pays → escrow holds → work delivered → funds released*
VO: "Secure escrow. No trust issues."
*Visual: Green checkmark animation, Stripe logo flash*

**[0:18-0:22] — IMPACT**
*Screen: Counter rapidly spinning up*
VO: "R50 million paid out. 10,000 professionals. One mission."
*Visual: Real faces of freelancers (diverse, professional)*

**[0:22-0:26] — THE VISION**
*Screen: Timeline swooping forward — 2026 → 2031*
VO: "One million jobs by 2031."
*Visual: Africa map lighting up country by country*

**[0:26-0:30] — CTA**
*Screen: FreelanceSkills logo + URL*
VO: "Your skills. Your stage. Join now."
*Text: freelanceskills.net*
*Visual: QR code fades in bottom-right*
