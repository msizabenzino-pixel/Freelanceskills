## FreelanceSkills.net Hits R50 Million Milestone, Connecting Over 10,000 South African Professionals with Global Clients

**Cape Town, South Africa — March 2026**

FreelanceSkills (Pty) Ltd (CIPC Reg: 2026/070509/09), the AI-powered freelance marketplace headquartered in Tableview, Cape Town, today announced it has surpassed R50 million in total platform transactions, a landmark achievement for South Africa's gig economy.

### Key Highlights

- **R50 Million+** in total freelancer payouts processed through secure Stripe escrow
- **10,000+** verified professionals across 12+ categories including software development, plumbing, electrical work, design, photography, and tutoring
- **4,200+** completed projects with a 4.9-star average rating
- **AI-Powered Matching** connecting the right talent with the right jobs using proprietary algorithms analyzing skills, location, availability, and past performance
- **Zero Disputes Unresolved** — 100% dispute resolution rate through the platform's AI mediation and admin escalation system

### Founder's Statement

"When we started FreelanceSkills, the vision was simple: give every South African professional — from a plumber in Soweto to a developer in Sandton — the tools to compete globally," said **Bernet**, Founder & CEO. "Hitting R50 million proves that the demand is real. We're not just building a marketplace; we're building economic infrastructure for Africa."

### Platform Capabilities

FreelanceSkills.net offers:

- **AI Suite**: Job matching, proposal generation, CV parsing, earnings forecasting, and contract generation powered by GPT-4
- **Secure Escrow**: Stripe-powered payment protection holding funds until work is verified and approved
- **Upskilling Academy**: Free and premium courses with certificates to help freelancers level up
- **Mobile App**: Full-featured React Native app with biometric auth, push notifications, and offline capability
- **2031 Vision**: Blockchain credentials, NFT badges, green impact scoring, DAO governance, and quantum-safe encryption on the roadmap

### The Road to 1 Million Jobs by 2031

FreelanceSkills has set an ambitious target: facilitate **1 million job opportunities by 2031**. The company's 8-phase roadmap includes:

1. **2024-2025**: Foundation & growth (marketplace launch, AI matching)
2. **2026**: AI-powered everything (contract generation, earnings forecasting)
3. **2027**: Blockchain integration (verifiable credentials, NFT certifications)
4. **2028**: Global expansion (12+ languages, 15+ currencies, African partnerships)
5. **2029**: Metaverse job fairs and AR portfolio previews
6. **2030**: Full sustainability scoring and carbon offset partnerships
7. **2031**: Vision complete — 1 million jobs, global impact

### Industry Context

South Africa's unemployment rate stands at 32.1%, with youth unemployment exceeding 60%. The gig economy represents a R45 billion opportunity in sub-Saharan Africa, growing at 33% annually. FreelanceSkills is positioned at the intersection of technology and economic empowerment.
