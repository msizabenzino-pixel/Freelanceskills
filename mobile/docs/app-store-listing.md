## Description
FreelanceSkills is South Africa's leading AI-powered freelance marketplace, designed to connect talented professionals with businesses and opportunities across the continent. Our platform leverages advanced AI to match skills with projects, ensuring the perfect fit for every job.

### Key Features:
- **AI-Powered Matching**: Our smart algorithms analyze your profile and preferences to recommend the most relevant jobs and talent.
- **Secure Payments**: Integrated Stripe payments with escrow-like protection ensure freelancers get paid and clients get quality work.
- **Global Opportunities**: Access a wide range of projects from local startups to international enterprises.
- **Skill Verification**: Stand out with verified badges and credentials that prove your expertise.
- **Integrated Academy**: Upskill yourself with curated courses and resources to stay ahead in your field.
- **Real-time Collaboration**: Seamless messaging, file sharing, and video calls to keep projects on track.

Whether you're a freelancer looking for your next big project or a business seeking top-tier talent, FreelanceSkills provides the tools you need to succeed in the modern digital economy.
